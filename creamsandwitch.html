Cream Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients

  • tablespoon virgin olive oil
  • 10 slices bread slices
  • 3 tablespoon cubed cottage cheese
  • 1 tablespoon green chilli
  • salt as required
  • 2 tablespoon garlic mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup hung curd
  • 2 tablespoon red bell pepper
  • 1 tablespoon coriander leaves

Procedure

Step 1 Make a Hung spread

  • Toast cottage cheese cubes in 1 tsp oil. In a mixing bowl, add hung curd, cottage cheese, bell peppers, green chilli, coriander leaves, salt and black pepper together.

Step 2 Toast the bread

  • Toast the bread pieces on both sides until crisp and golden. Spread the garlic mayonnaise and line up on a chopping board.

Step 3 Fill in the stuffing

  • Evenly spread 2-3 tbsp of creamy stuffing on one bread slice and cover it with another. Serve with ketchup or mustard sauce.
