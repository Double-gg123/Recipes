Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 tablespoon powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/2 cup of all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon refined oil
  • 1 egg

Procedure

Step 1 Prepare the batter

  • In a bowl, mash the banana using the back of a spoon or a fork. In another bowl, break an egg and whisk well until it gets frothy. Combine the all-purpose flour with baking powder and salt in a large bowl. Then add the beaten egg mixture, sugar, oil, milk and add the mashed banana. Mix well to prepare a batter

Step 2 Make pancake cereal

  • Now keep a non-stick tawa on medium flame. Pour drops of pancake batter into it. The pancake drops should be coin-shaped. Let them cook from one side and then flip. Make more such coin-sized pancakes. (Remember- Don't add oil to the tawa before adding the batter)

Step 3 Ready to be served

  • Add the pancake cereal to a bowl. Garnish with honey, blueberries, banana or any other fruit. Serve!
